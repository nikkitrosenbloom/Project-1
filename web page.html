What's the Common Thread Among 10 Years of Plane Crashes?

By: Nikki Rosenbloom

But there has been an ostensible increase in the number of crashes over the past few months. The deadliest crash in the last decade happened just one month ago, on June 12th, 2025.

The Bureau of Aviation Accident Archives sorts plane crashes around the world from the 1960s to today. The database, based in Switzerland, organizes the flights by date, operator, aircraft type, location, and fatalities.
